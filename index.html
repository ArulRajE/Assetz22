About

Builder

Assetz Property Group was founded in year 2006 and is headquartered in Singapore. It has established itself as a leading property developer in Bangalore, India. Its a front-runner amidst multinational developers in India with over 10 million square feet under development. Its business is basically divided into four verticals: Commercial, Residential, Warehousing and Fund Management.

Assetz Group has built two world-class IT Tech parks in Bangalore: Vrindavan Tech Park (Now Embassy Tech Village) – India’s first Platinum LEED certified IT SEZ and Global Technology Park. And, in its Residential portfolio includes Ten projects in prominent locations across Bangalore.
